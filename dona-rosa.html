Dona Rosa,
E-commerce App

My Role

I was the UI/UX Designer of this project.

Main Tasks

  • Serve users through tangible solutions
  • Understand cognitive psychology, heuristics and research;
    and the behaviour and motivations of users
  • Create mockups and flows for the user interface
  • Create a style guide

The Challenge

Re-design Dona Rosa's website to improve user experience through the shopping process, and improve user engagement.

The Outcome

App for purchasing products from Dona Rosa bio farm, providing users with a seamless, transparent, and enjoyable shopping experience. By incorporating an intuitive interface, detailed product information, and customizable orders. Additionally, features such as recipes, customer support, and sustainable practices further enhance the user experience and build a strong connection between users and the farm's mission of promoting organic and sustainable agriculture.

The Process

1. Research
2. Synthesis
3. Ideation
4. Design

1. Research

I started brainstorming to explore the major problems of the previous website. For this I tried to answer this questions: what problems users are experiencing while using the website? Who is experiencing them and why are they problems to begin with? what benefits could the possible solutions bring to users and to the company as well?

Some of the points to focus are: (1) User-friendly Product Catalog; (2) Search and Filtering Capabilities; (3) Seamless Ordering Process.
The goal is to facilitate a seamless and enjoyable process of sourcing high-quality bio food.

2. Synthesis

Here we started generating ideas - such as functionalities, target groups and information architecture.

The functionalities of the app are based on the target audience, business goals, and the unique requirements of the farm.

3. Ideation

Wireframes

Then I proceeded to create an initial version of the app screens. This was important to establish the bridge between conceptualizing the app's structure and visualizing its interface. It helped clarify the app's functionality and guide the subsequent stages of UI design.
